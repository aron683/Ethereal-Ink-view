Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a professional website cost?

Basic business websites start from R500! This includes mobile-friendly design, contact forms, and basic setup. E-commerce sites start from R800. WhatsApp us for exact pricing based on your specific needs.

How long does it take to get my website live?

Most basic websites are ready within 24-48 hours after you provide your business information and photos. More complex sites with e-commerce may take 3-5 days.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Absolutely! All our websites are 100% mobile-responsive and look perfect on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Over 60% of your customers will visit from mobile devices.

What information do I need to provide?

Just your business name, contact details, services/products, photos of your work, and any special offers. We handle all the technical work - no computer skills needed!

Can I update my website myself?

Yes! We provide simple tools to update your content, or we can handle updates for you at a small fee. Many of our clients prefer us to manage their websites for them.
